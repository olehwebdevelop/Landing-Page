Apollo 11: The First Step on the Moon

In 1969, humanity achieved one of its greatest milestones — landing on the Moon. This mission changed history and proved that impossible goals can become reality. Explore the journey, the crew, and the legacy of Apollo 11.

About the Mission

Apollo 11 was the first space mission that successfully landed humans on the Moon. It was led by NASA and launched on July 16, 1969.

After traveling over 380,000 kilometers through space, the spacecraft reached lunar orbit and prepared for landing. This mission marked a defining moment in science, technology, and human exploration.
